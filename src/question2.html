Welcome to the IT Factory course, a comprehensive program designed to arm you with skills in the most cutting-edge and in-demand areas of IT.

Application development

Are you the next developer behind a new Wakeout, Zoom, or WhatsApp? This program equips you with the tools and knowledge to make that ambition a reality. From day one, you'll start learning to code and build a solid foundation in IT. As you progress, you'll specialize in a pathway of your choice.

Artificial Intelligence

Embark on your IT journey with a focus on artificial intelligence, starting with a solid knowledge base, then enhancing your skills through specialized paths such as Python, Java, and machine learning. During your final year, complete a 13-week internship locally or internationally. This education prepares you to be an AI innovation lead or data scientist, with a high probability of securing a job within three months post graduation.

Cloud and Cyber Security

This program transforms your fascination for computer networks and systems into a career-ready expertise, starting with a solid IT foundation and moving towards specialization in Linux, Microsoft services, and data center technology. Through practical lessons, group work, and projects, you'll master firewalls, pentesting, security operations, and more.

Internet of Things

Curious about the potential of Internet of Things (IoT), you will start with a broad foundation in IT, learning about computer networks, Python, Java, and web design. From the second year, you'll dive into IoT, understanding how to connect devices and sensors to the internet, exchange and interpret data, and work with smart home technology.
